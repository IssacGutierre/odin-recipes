Ziti Pasta

Pasta that tastes like it was imported from Italy!

This pasta has become a staple in my monthly meals. Its extremely simply to make, but tastes so good!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ziti pasta
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • garlic powder
  • salt and pepper
  • mozzarela cheese
  • 2 jars of maranaira sause

Steps

  1. Place beef inside a stove pot on medium to high heat. season it with garlic, salt and pepper
  2. Once beef is done cooking, add in the 2 jars of maranaira sause and mix well.
  3. Prep the ziti noodles according to the box instructions.
  4. Once the noodles are done, mix everything togther well. Apply a thin coat of meat sauce on an oven safe pan.
  5. load up the pasta onto the pan in 3 layers. Each layer contaning a coat of mozzarela cheese.
  6. Place the pan inside the oven for 20 minutes at 400 degrees and Enjoy your chessy creation!
