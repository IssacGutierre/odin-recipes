Mexican Corn in a Cup

A differnt way to enjoy corn!

You can find this delicacy in the eye of morning flea markets and in front of hispanic convienant stores. The warm flavours going down into your stomach on a freezing morning brings much joy!

Ingredients

  • canned corn
  • mayo
  • limes
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • chopped cilantro
  • chili powder

Steps

  1. Bring 1 cup of water to a boil. When the water is boiling, add corn with lid. Simmer corn for 3-5 minutes or until corn is tender.
  2. When corn is done cooking, drain well and place inside a cup. Add mayo until well combined.
  3. Top corn with cheese, cilantro, chili powder and lime.
