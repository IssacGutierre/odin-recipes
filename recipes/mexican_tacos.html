Mexican tacos

Authentic, Delicious Mexican Tacos!

This recipe is a staple across all of Mexicao. The protein can be mixed and substituted with many different types! You can also choose a mariad of differnt toppings.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Flour Tortillas
  • Diced steak(Carne Asada)
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • limes
  • Guacamole(optional)

Steps

  1. Heat up 2 pans at medium heat, and add olive oil to both.
  2. Place tortillas in one pan and cook until a couple browns spots appear. (remember to flip them a couple times!)
  3. Place diced up, seasoned steak into the other pan and cook until its well done.
  4. Once both the meat and tortillas are done, place the meat onto the tortillas.
  5. Relish the tacos with diced onion and sliced cilantro.
  6. Add a splash of lime juice and enjoy!
